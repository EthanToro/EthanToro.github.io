EMERGENCY SERVICE 24/7

Our extensive crew of trained master technicians are automatically dispatched 24/7, each equipped with a service vehicle, tools, and parts to get your equipment back up and running, so you can get back into production as quickly as possible.

We inventory thousands of parts common to most brands such as: Sullair, Atlas Copco, Ingersoll-Rand, Chicago Pneumatic, Kaeser, Champion, Gardner Denver...and many more!

In the event your part is not in stock, our vast repository of vendors enables us acquire your part as quickly as possible, or if your part is no longer available we can retrofit a comparable part to replace the original and keep your system up and running!

SERVICES

The Corey Compressor, Inc. Service Technician will perform an overall visual inspection of the machine condition. In order to ensure a comprehensive program we will also include the following routine checks, electrical checks and alignments:

  • Change oil as needed (synthetic or mineral based oil as required)
  • Inspect belt tension make any belt adjustment
  • Noise inspection and evaluation
  • Inspect electrical connections
  • Take amp, temperature and pressure readings
  • Replacement of air filter
  • Replace Separator
  • Inspect / clean drain valves
  • Perform safety checks
  • General cleaning of coolers
  • Inspect auxiliary equipment for proper operation
